Fried Chicken

Description

Fried chicken is an extremely popular and easy dish that is great for almost any occasion. It is easy and fun to make and it will have you coming back for seconds!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Chicken Wings
  • Chicken Thighs
  • Chicken Drumsticks
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Corn Starch
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Vegetable Oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix buttermilk, salt, and pepper. Add chicken to the mix and let sit in the fridge for 4 hours.
  2. After chicken sits, add vegetable oil to skillet and heat to 350 degrees.
  3. In another bowl combine flour, corn starch, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and mix.
  4. Now remove chicken pieces from the buttermilk mix and coat in the flour mix.
  5. Once all chicken is coated in the flour mix, place chicken into heated vegetable oil for about 14 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees, turning halfway through
  6. Remove chicken from oil, and let rest for 10 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
