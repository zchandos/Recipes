Pepperoni Pizza

Description

The Italians know how to get it done! Pepperoni Pizza is a instant classic that is sure not to disappoint. This is a super flexible recipe that allows you to experiment and try different things depending on what you like.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Instructions

  1. If not already, flatten your pizza dough down to a size and thickness of your choice.
  2. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce across your dough in a even layer, with the amount of your choice.
  4. Now spread the mozzarella cheese over the top of the tomato sauce, with the amount of your choice.
  5. Next spread your pepperoni and any other toppings over the top of the cheese, once again with the amount of your choice.
  6. Now place your pizza into the over for 8-15 minutes. This time will vary depending on how large, thick, and how much toppings your pizza contains. Go until your crust is golden brown.
  7. Remove pizza from the oven, and let rest for about 5 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
